The Admin's Experience (David at Rapid Restore)

At the Rapid Restore office, David gets an alert on his Reclaim360 Admin Dashboard. Sarah's new job appears. He doesn't just see her name and address; he sees the photos, the video, and the AI's initial report. The AI has already flagged the job as "medium priority" and estimated the type of equipment that will be needed. David looks at the schedule on the dashboard and sees that his technician, Mike, is finishing a job nearby. He assigns Sarah's job to Mike with a single click.

Transparency in Action

When Mike arrives, he does his work. As he completes key steps (like water extraction, setting up drying fans), he updates the status on his device. Sarah sees these updates in real-time in her portal: "Status Update: Water extraction complete." and "Status Update: Professional drying equipment is now running." Over the next few days, Sarah never has to call and ask, "What's going on?" She can simply open the Reclaim360 portal and see the job status, view photos of the progress, and communicate with the team via a built-in chat if she has questions.
